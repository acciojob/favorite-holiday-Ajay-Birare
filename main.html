Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

  1. Snorkeling in the Coral Reefs - The Maldives has some of the most stunning coral reefs in the world, and snorkeling is a must-do activity.
  2. Relaxing on the Beaches - The beaches in the Maldives are pristine and perfect for sunbathing and relaxing.
  3. Island Hopping - The Maldives is made up of over 1,000 islands, each with its own unique beauty and charm.
  4. Dolphin Watching - The Maldives is home to a variety of marine life, including playful dolphins that can be spotted on boat tours.
  5. Scuba Diving - For the more adventurous, scuba diving in the Maldives offers the opportunity to explore underwater caves and swim with colorful fish and sea turtles.
  • Mas huni - A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Garudhiya - A flavorful fish soup made with spices and coconut milk.
  • Bis keemiya - Deep-fried pastry filled with spicy tuna and potatoes.
  • Fihunu mas - Grilled fish marinated with spices and served with rice and vegetables.
  • Coconut water - A refreshing drink that is easily available throughout the Maldives.
